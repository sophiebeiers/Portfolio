Bullying Over Time

Over time, roughly a quarter of all middle and high school students have experienced bullying of some kind.

Before 2017

Bullying had been on the rise by roughly one percentage point each year between 2014, 2015, and 2016.


2017

After Trump's election in late 2016, however, the percentage of students who reported having been bullied that school year rose three percentage points. Regression analysis indicated that in 2017, students were 30% more likely to experience bullying in 2017 than in the three years prior.
