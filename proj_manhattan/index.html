Old & New NYC Buildings

Buildings colored by date built in the Upper West Side, Upper East Side & Harlem areas of Manhattan, NYC. Most were built prior to 1900, but newer buildings can be seen emerging in gentrifying areas.


Map used NYC's Pluto data and design inspiration from BKLNYR.com. Produced using Javascript Leaflet and finished in Illustrator.
